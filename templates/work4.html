Recruitment

As a Recruitment Consultant I was involved with Business Development and Recruiting (Headhunting and Sourcing Top end Professionals) for the Mining (CA Mining) and Tech (DataTech Recruitment) Industries across Africa.
Business Development: Sourcing new business through research, sales calls (both warm and cold calls) and client meetings, to develop new client relationships and maintain existing ones.
Recruitment: Management of the entire recruitment process from development and nurturing of clients through to candidate placement - this entails sourcing candidates with key profiles and skill sets for the mining industry, arranging interviews, conducting references and criminal / qualification checks, mediating offer negotiations and ensuring a smooth onboarding process. As well as managing the expectations of both candidate and client throughout the process.
My role entailed using various CRM and ATS software daily, as well as making use of various databases to source candidates and research and follow up on leads and opportunities. We were encouraged to treat our desk as our own business and so I also created several social media campaigns to enhance my personal brand as a recruiter, making use of SEO, copywriting skills as well as keyword planning and analytics tools.
